residency /

Bashar Suleiman

period: 2018-2019

hometown: Amman, Jordan

Bashar Suleiman, aka lil asaf, spent most of 2018 and 2019 with us in Cairo—building, experimenting, and fully merging into the city’s raw creative flow. From making beats around the clock in our house studio to late-night gigs and sharing sounds with local heads, he became part of the scene’s rhythm.

His stay was packed with sessions, beats, and collaborations with Egyptian artists. He shaped mixes that moved through clubs, rooftops, and underground spaces. He co-created Pink Tape with American producer and DJ Drummer B, also in residency, and released it on our label. He also put out Throwbacks, a solo EP of beats.

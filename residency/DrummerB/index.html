Selected previous residents:

HIZZ / residency

Drummer B

In 2018, Drummer B joined HIZZ’s residency program in Cairo. During this period, he collaborated with fellow resident artist Bashar Suleiman to produce the album Pink Tape. This project involved sampling old cassette tapes from HIZZ’s archives, blending them with contemporary beats rooted in both Detroit and the Middle East. The album was released on Bandcamp and was also produced as a physical pink cassette tape by the HIZZ team in Cairo.

Valley of Kings – Detroit to Cairo

On his subsequent visit, Drummer B worked on his solo album titled Valley of Kings – Detroit to Cairo. This ten-track album continued the exploration of HIZZ’s cassette archives, reimagining lost sounds and field recordings while featuring collaborations with Detroit artists such as Pierre Anthony, Jon Dixon, and Umar Bin Hassan. The album was released digitally and as a limited-edition cassette, handcrafted by the HIZZ team.

Visual Work

During his time in Cairo, Drummer B also collaborated with Egyptian filmmaker Malak El Sawi on a music video that captured moments from the project. In addition, he produced a series of self-directed MPC beat-making videos with support from the HIZZ team — documenting his creative process in various locations across the city and offering a visual companion to the sonic bridge he built between Detroit and Cairo.

residency /

Drummer B

period: 2018-2019

hometown: Detroit, USA

Drummer B joined our residency in Cairo in 2018, where he and Bashar Suleiman co-created Pink Tape—sampling HIZZ’s old cassettes and blending Detroit beats with regional textures. The album came out on Bandcamp and as a pink cassette handmade in Cairo.

On a later visit, he made Valley of Kings – Detroit to Cairo, a solo LP rooted in archive sounds and field recordings, featuring Pierre Anthony, Jon Dixon, and Umar Bin Hassan. released digitally on tape.

During his stay, Drummer B also shot a music video with filmmaker Malak El Sawi and documented beat-making sessions across the city with the HIZZ team—building a visual record of his process and the bridge between both cities.
